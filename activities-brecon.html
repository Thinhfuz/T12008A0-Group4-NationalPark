Mountain Bikers


For beginner mountain bikers through to seasoned downhill pro’s, BikePark Wales will offer you an incredible experience unlike anything else in the UK.

We have the longest green and blue graded descents in the UK offering unbelievable fun and a perfect opportunity to progress for beginner and intermediate riders.

With over 40 trails to choose from even the best riders will spend several blissfully adrenaline filled days exploring our hugely diverse trail network.

The concept is simple – picture a ski resort, remove the snow and replace the pistes with an array of sweeping flowy bike trails meandering to the bottom of the mountain and you are close. Add a bunch of adrenaline and big sprinkle of fun and you are pretty much there. BikePark Wales is an exhilarating way to spend your day in the Welsh mountains.

Our series of lift assisted trails for riders of all abilities offer the ultimate riding experience. With a mix of incredible all weather roller coaster trails and some of the best natural Singletrack the UK has to offer, we challenge you to suppress the silly grin on your face at the bottom of each run.


Cantref Riding Centre


Cantref Riding Centre is situated in the Brecon Beacons, South Wales and has been established for over 40 years ago. We offer horse riding for all ages and abilities over 4 years old. You can choose from an introductory 20 minute lesson, a relaxing hour’s ride around the farm, a half days ride or a full days ride up onto the Brecon Beacons National Park taking in the spectacular scenery.

At Cantref Riding Centre our experienced guides make every effort to ensure that our riders are well looked after and cared for from start to finish allowing an enjoyable, stress free day to be had by all. All riders will be equipped with a riding hat and receive a basic safety demonstration before being introduced to their horse or pony and leaving the yard.

Our knowledge and experience over the years has led to many return visitors. Cantref Riding Centre welcomes families and groups to come and enjoy horse riding in the Brecon Beacons. We are licensed and approved by many of the major equine organisations local council, W.T.R.A and A.A.L.A.

Please note our weight limit is 16 stone and that children must be over 4 years old to ride a pony at Cantref Riding Centre.


CANTREF ADVENTURE FARM


Come and visit our great children’s adventure farm park set in the heart of the Brecon Beacons National Park! We have a large heated indoor play area which is also adult friendly so you can play with your children or you can sit back and relax with a hot drink and homemade cake from our farmhouse cafe. Enjoy looking at our small animals in our Pet Barn and wander outside to explore the larger animals here at Cantref. There are live animal shows throughout the day, plus all our great family outdoor attractions are available too! Cantref is a great day out for the whole family.

The shows include Bottle Feeding the Lambs, the Mr Ev’s Show, Pony Rides*, Tractor & Trailer Rides and the fantastic Pig Racing to name but a few! (*Pony rides are an additional £2.50 and children must be 93cm and over to ride at Cantref).

Other attractions also include the Young Tractor Drivers Challenge, Paddle Boats, Europe Longest Sledge Ride, Brand new crazy golf as well as much more – All of which is included within the admission price! On your way out we have an onsite gift shop where you can purchase a souvenir of your special day here at Cantref – Brecon’s Biggest Adventure!

Please visit our website www.cantref.com for our daily programme & find out what exciting Events we have planned for 2020!
